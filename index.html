Roll Fer Yer Life Matey!!

The objective of this game is to roll two dice and get a higher value than your opponent.
It's not as simple as just rolling the dice though. You will have a chance to guess what the roll will be
You have to guess respectively! Your guess for the first die won't count towards the second.
The exact same rules apply to your opponent. Except he doesn't get walk the plank if he loses...
